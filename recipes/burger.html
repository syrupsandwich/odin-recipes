burger

Description

This recipe instructs how to make a tasty burger. It will remind you of your childhood school lunch. Here are the things you will need for making the burger.

  • 2 large wheat buns
  • 1 large fully cooked meat patty
  • pickle relish
  • an onion
  • ketchup
  • ranch

steps to make the burger:

  1. take one meat patty out and onto a plate
  2. add a spoon of the pickle relish to the bottom bun.
  3. wrap the patty with plastic wrap
  4. cut a slice of onion to about 2mm thin
  5. place the slice of onion on the bottom bun
  6. heat the patty in the microwave until it is hot
  7. take the plastic wrap off of the patty
  8. place the patty on the bottom bun
  9. add a tablespoon of ketchup on top of the patty
  10. add a tablespoon of ranch on top of the patty.
  11. mix and spread the sauce (optional)
  12. put the top bun over the sauce
